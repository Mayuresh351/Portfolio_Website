IOT in Heat Transfer Machinery

June 2022 - July 2022
Research Internship

The internship was conducted under the guidance of Dr. Nitin P. Gulhane. An IOT based project developed to monitor heat transfer machinery like Double Pipe Heat Exchanger. A prototype for the system is designed which alerts the user if the temperature of the system crosses the threshold by the means of email or SMS. The user will also be able to take the necessary action using the application.
The project also uses Cloud Computing and API services for its working. Bolt IOT is the hardware module used for the purpose.
